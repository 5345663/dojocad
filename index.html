What is this?

This is a half-assed Dojo builder. It's still a work in progress.

Getting started

The starter Clan Hall is already added by default.

Click the Add Room button to select a category and room to add. The drag and click where the room should be placed.

Navigation

Click anywhere in the grid area and drag to move around.

This includes clicking on a room, as long as you don't hold too long before dragging (see Moving And Rotation).

Editing

Adding a Room

Click the Add Room button to show a menu of room categories. Click one of these categories to show a menu of rooms. Click one of those rooms to add one under your cursor. Move the cursor where you want the room and click to drop it.

If you have already added a room once, then the Add Room menu will contain a quick option for that room at the top, before the categories.

Moving and Rotation

Click and hold on a room for half a second, then dragging will move the room rather than dragging the view.

Click and release on a room without dragging will rotate it 90 degrees.

Hold down Shift and click on a room to start moving it immediately, without having to hold it for half a second.

Hold down Shift while dragging a room to snap its position to the nearest 8 meters on the grid. Otherwise the room's position is snapped to the nearest meter.

Note that Shift-dragging a newly created room also snaps.

Deleting a Room

Holding down Alt and click on a room will delete it.
